Grapefruit-Orange Punch
(Makes 4 Servings)
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 2 Cups Canned Grapefruit-Orange Juice
  1. Combine sugar and water; boil 3 minutes.
  2. Let cool.
  3. Add grapefruit-orange juice
  4. Pour over ice in glasses and serve.
► For different variations, use different fruit juices ◄

Lemonade
(Makes 6-8 Servings)
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Water
  • Rind of 2 Lemons, Cut Into Pieces
  • 1 Cup Lemon Juice (5-6 Lemons)
  • 4 Cups Ice Water
  1. Combine sugar, 1 Cup of non-iced water, and rinds in saucepan.
  2. Stir over low heat until sugar is dissolved
  3. Boil about 7 minutes.
  4. Let cool completely.
  5. Add lemon juice and iced water.
  6. Remove rinds, stir lemonade.
  7. Pour over ice in pither or tall glasses.
Variations:
  • Quick Lemonade:
    Squeeze lemons, add sugar, water, and ice to taste

  • Minted Lemonade:
    Place bruised mint leaves in bottom of glasses before adding lemonade. Garnish each glass with a sprig of mint.

  • Berry Lemonade:
    To each glass of lemonade add 2 tbsp of crushed berries (fresh or canned). Berries can be raspberries, strawberries, blackberries, or blueberries. Garnish with whole berries and serve over ice.

  • Grape Lemonade:
    Combine equal portions of grape juice and lemonade.

Orange Jupiter
(Makes 4 Servings)
  • 1 Cup Powdered Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 3 Cups Orange Juice (Canned or Fresh)
  • ½ Cup Sugar
  • 9 Ice Cubes
  1. Blend all ingredients, except ice cubes.
  2. Add 1 ice cube at a time, blending after each.
  3. Serve cold.

Orangeade
(Makes 6-8 Servings)
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 2 Cups Orange Juice
  • 4 Cups Iced Water
  • ¼ Cup Lemon Juice
  1. Combine 1 Cup of water (non-iced)and sugar in saucepan.
  2. Heat to boiling and boil for 3 minutes.
  3. Let cool.
  4. Add orange juice, lemon juice, and iced water.
  5. Stir and serve.

Tomato Juice Cocktail
(Makes 6 Servings)
  • 3 Cups Tomato Juice
  • 3 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Finely Grated Onion
  • ¼ tsp Celery Salt (Optional)
  1. Combine all ingredients.
  2. Serve either hot or cold.
  3. Float lemon slice on top to garnish. (Optional)
