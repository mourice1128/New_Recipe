Rice Pudding
  • ⅓ Cup Cooked White Rice
  • 1⅓ Cup Milk
  • 2 Tbsp Sugar
  • ⅛ tsp Salt
  • 1 Egg, Beaten
  • ¼ tsp Vanilla
  • ¼ tsp Butter
  • Dash of Nutmeg (Optional)
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350°.
  2. Scald milk and remove from heat.
  3. Add rice, sugar, and salt.
  4. Mix well and slowly add the egg, vanilla, and butter.
  5. Pour into small baking dish, then sprinkle with nutmeg (if desired)
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes, stirring at least once after 20 minuts.

Broccoli Chicken & Rice Bake
  • 1 Can of Chicken & Broccoli Soup
  • ½ Cup Water
  • ¼ Cup Uncooked White Rice
  • ⅛ tsp Pepper
  • 2 Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breasts
  1. Pre-heat oven to 375°.
  2. Mix soup, water, and rice.
  3. Pour into small casserole dish.
  4. Place chicken on top.
  5. Cover and bake for 45 minutes.

Bread Pudding
  • 1 Cup Cubed Bread
  • 1 tbsp Butter, Melted
  • ¼ tsp Cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp Raisins
  • 2 Eggs, Beaten
  • ¼ Cup Sugar
  • ¾ tsp Vanilla
  • ⅛ tsp Salt
  • 1 Cup Hot Milk
  • Pinch Ground Nutmeg
  1. Pre-heat oven to 375°.
  2. Combine bread, butter, cinnamon, and raisins.
  3. Mix well, then pour into a lightly buttered baking dish.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat together eggs, sugar, vanilla, and salt.
  5. Add milk and pour over the bread mixture.
  6. Sprinkle with nutmeg and bake for 25 minutes.

10 Minute Apple Crisp
FOR COATING MIXTURE:
  • 3 tbsp Sugar
  • ½ tsp Cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp Nutmeg
  • ½ tsp Cornstarch
  • 1 Apple
FOR DRIZZLING MIXTURE:
  • 1¼ tbsp Butter
  • 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Oats
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • ½ tsp Cinnamon
  1. Peel and slice Apple.
  2. Mix together sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cornstarch for the COATING MIXTURE.
  3. Roll apple slices in the COATING MIXTURE until completely coated.
  4. Place in a microwave-safe dish, cover, and microwave for 4 minutes.
  5. While microwaving the COATING MIXTURE, cook together in a frying pan, the DRIZZLING MIXTURE ingredients.
  6. Pour DRIZZLING MIXTURE over the apples and return to the microwave for an additional 30 seconds.
  7. Top with 1 scoop of ice cream. (Optional)

Chili Verde Smothered Bean & Cheese Buritos
  • 1 Pkg. Pork Gravy Mix
    (Prepared According to Directions)
  • ½ Lb. Diced Pork
  • Tortillas
  • ¼ Cup Salsa (Desired Spiciness)
  • 1 tsp Taco Seasoning
  • 1 Can Refried Beans
  • Small Pkg. Grated Cheese
  1. Prepare gravy mix according to directions on package.
  2. Add pork, salsa, and tco seasoning to gravy and stir until hot.
  3. Heat refried beans in microwave, until hot
  4. Fill tortilla with desired amount of refried beans, cheese, and chili verde.
  5. Roll tortillas into burito, pour chili verde on top.
  6. Cover with additional cheese.
  7. Microwave until cheese is melted.

French Bread Pizza
  • 1 Loaf of French Bread
  • Prepared Spaghetti Sauce
Some of the following ingredients
(depending on the taste desired):
  • Beef Crumbles
  • Sausage Crumbles
  • Pepperoni
  • Olives
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Banana Peppers
  • Green Peppers
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Bacon
  • Jalepeños                                 
  1. Pre-heat oven to 400°.
  2. Slice bread in ½. (Like if you were making a sub sandwich)
  3. Cut into the length desired for your pizza.
  4. Cover with sauce and desired toppings.
  5. Bake for 5-7 minutes.
