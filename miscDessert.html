RaeShel's Horton Family S'More Treats
  • 16 oz Bag Marshmallows
  • 12 oz Chocolate Chips
  • 8 oz Bag Marshmallows
  • ⅓ Cup Karo® Dark Syrup
  • 5 tbsp Butter
  • 12 oz Golden Graham® Cereal
  1. In large bowl, combine 16 oz bag of marshmallows, chocolate chips, syrup, and butter
  2. Microwave in 1 minutes intervals, until melted; stiring after each interval
  3. In a seperate large bowl, combine Cereal and 8 oz bag of marshmallows
  4. Pour liquid mixture over cereal mix; continue to mix until well blended
  5. Enjoy!

Creamy Banana Pudding
(Makes 1 Serving)
  • 1 4-Serving Size Banana Cream Pudding Mix
  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 2 Cups Vanilla Wafers
  • 2 Lg. Bananas, Sliced Into ½" Pieces
  • Caramel Sauce (Optional)
  1. In large bowl, combine pudding mix and milk.
  2. Whisk for 2 minutes.
  3. Refridgerate for 5 minutes.
  4. In 4 bowls, spoon some of the pudding, followed by some of the wafers and banana slices.
  5. Repeat with remianing ingredients, drizzle with caramel sauce (Optional).
