Chocolate Brownies
  • 1 Cup Butter (2 Sticks or ½ lb.)
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 4 Eggs
  • ¾ Cup Cocoa
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • ½ tsp Baking Powder
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • 1 Cup Chopped Nuts (Optional)
  • Creamy Brownie Frosting (Optional)
  1. Heat oven to 350°.
  2. Grease 9x13x2" baking pan.
  3. Place butter in a lg. microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on MEDIUM (50%) for 2-2½ minutes or until melted.
  4. Stir in sugar and vanilla.
  5. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well with spoon after each addition.
  6. Add cocoa; beat until well blended.
  7. Add flour, baking powder, and salt; beat well.
  8. Stir in nuts (Optional)
  9. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  10. Bake 30-35 minutes or until brownies begin to pull away from the sides.
  11. Cool completely in pan on wire rack.
  12. Prepare frosting and frost (Optional)

Maureen Brandon's Brownies
  • 4 oz Baker's Unsweetened Chocolate
  • ⅔ Cup Oil
  • 2 Cups Sugar
  • 4 Eggs
  • ¾Cup Cocoa
  • 1½ Cup Flour
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Cup Chopped Nuts (Optional)
  1. Heat oven to 350°.
  2. Grease 9x13" baking pan.
  3. Place Chocolate in microwave until melted
  4. In a large bowl, mix all other ingredients, until well blended
  5. Pour into greased baking pan
  6. Bake 26-28 minutes
  7. Bake 30-35 minutes or until brownies begin to pull away from the sides.
  8. Cool completely in pan on wire rack.
  9. Prepare frosting and frost (Optional)
